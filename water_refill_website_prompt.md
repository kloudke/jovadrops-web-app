# Water Refill Station Website Prompt

Act as a senior UX designer, conversion rate optimization (CRO) expert, and full-stack web developer. Your task is to design and generate a **high-converting, modern website** for a **Water Refill Station business** targeting urban and peri-urban customers.

The goal of the website is to:
- Increase water refill orders
- Drive mobile app downloads (primary CTA)
- Build trust and credibility
- Educate users about water quality and convenience
- Convert visitors into repeat customers

## 1. BUSINESS CONTEXT
Business Type: Water Refill Station  

Target Audience:
- Households
- Offices and small businesses
- Apartment residents
- Health-conscious individuals

Key Selling Points:
- Clean, purified, safe drinking water
- Affordable pricing
- Convenient refill or delivery options
- Subscription plans available
- Mobile app for ordering

Primary Conversion Goal:
➡️ Drive users to download the mobile app

Secondary Goals:
- View products/pricing
- Contact or request delivery
- Learn about water quality process

## 2. WEBSITE STRUCTURE
Pages:
1. Home
2. Products / Pricing
3. How It Works
4. About Us
5. Contact
6. FAQ
7. Download App Page
